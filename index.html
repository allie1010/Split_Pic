The Friends Cast Doesn't Age!

Friends is one of the most beloved television shows of all time, running for ten seasons and even coming back for a huge reunion episode. The main characters: Rachel, Phoebe, Joey, Ross and Monica weasled their way into the hearts of anyone who watched. Now, all these years later, the show is still a success and the actors and actresses seem to look the same as they did back when they began the series in 1998.
